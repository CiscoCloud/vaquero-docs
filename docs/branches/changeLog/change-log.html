Change log

Vaquero container release history and change logs. All containers are served from bintray. We do not recommend using latest since it will be released once or day or more. Vaquero upgrades should be done from major version to major version, we do not provide backwards compatibility across more than 1 major release. Alpha(Pre 1.0) releases should be upgraded from minor release to minor release.
